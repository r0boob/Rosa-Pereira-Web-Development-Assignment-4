Cybersecurity Awareness

Why Awareness Matters

I believe that cybersecurity is an essential skill in today's world. The purpose of this course is to inform everyday internet users about the importance of security's best practices in the online world. But how do we get through to people who could not care less about cybersecurity? We try to make it fun through a series of interactive modules that help learners stay engaged. With help from technologies employed (such as Canva and Canvas LMS) and 10 carefully collected Scholarly Sources this course presents learners with practical knowledge for daily digital safety.

Highlights

  • Learners can earn badges for every module completed.
  • The course has interactive scenario-based challenges.
  • Users will be able to understand why awareness is important in the cyberspace.
  • Mitigation strategies for current attacks.
  • The course is desgined for different types of learners.
  • Anyone with the link will be able to register for the course for free.
