This website talks about my capstone project: an educational cybersecurity awareness course hosted on Canvas LMS and designed for beginners.

The course covers essential topics such as current threats, mitigation strategies, and best security practices. Modules are designed for beginners, so highly technical language is avoided, when inevitable, all concepts are explained completely. The cyber survival guide has interactive elements such as quizzes, scenario based challenges, reflection posts and users are able to earn badges for every module or challenge that they successfully complete.
